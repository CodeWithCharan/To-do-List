About

The MyToDo application is a simple and intuitive to-do list manager that helps you stay organized and manage your tasks effectively.

Features

  • Create tasks: Add new tasks to your to-do list.
  • Read tasks: View your existing tasks and their details.
  • Update tasks: Edit and modify task information.
  • Delete tasks: Remove tasks from your to-do list.

Usage

To use the MyToDo application, follow these steps:

  1. Create a task: Click on the "Add Task" button and fill in the task details.
  2. Read tasks: The tasks will be displayed in a table format on the home page.
  3. Update a task: Click on the "Edit" button next to a task and modify the task details.
  4. Delete a task: Click on the "Delete" button next to a task to remove it from your to-do list.

Stay organized and manage your tasks efficiently with MyToDo!
